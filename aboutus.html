About Deshmukh Classes

In the year 2012, a visionary saw a dream. A dream to impart quality education to every student
who would come seeking it. Through his persistent efforts, Prof. Sarvat Deshmukh realized his
dream and brought the Deshmukh Classes to life along of the organization. Deshmukh Classes
has evolved tremendously, both in their scale and the quality of education. Started from just 20
students in the first batch, the class today caters to thousands of students every year.
